Getting Around Mexico City?

There are many ways to get around the city. It has one of the largest subway networks in the world, as well as public transportation networks made up of thousands of buses,minibuses and vans and electric systems such as the Trolleybus, Light Rail, Cablelbus and cabs.

To facilitate seemless movement, the government of Mexico City implemented the use of a transportation card that allows users to use many different means of transportation and pay for all of them with the same card.
